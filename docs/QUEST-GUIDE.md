# Beds of Empires — Quest Guide

> *Your bed is your life. Protect it, or die trying.*

Welcome to **Beds of Empires**, a PvP modpack where players defend their bed, progress through Civilization-inspired tech trees, and destroy enemy beds to win. This guide covers all **399 quests** across **8 chapters**.

---

## How Quests Work

Open your **Quest Book** to see your available quests. Here's how to read the quest map:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Large hexagon | **Starting point** or **final achievement** — look here first |
| Small hexagon | **Rank milestone** — completing this promotes you and announces it server-wide |
| Circle | Normal quest — complete to unlock the next |
| Rounded square | Special item (glyphs, optional content) |
| Greyed out quest | **Locked** — complete its parent quest first |
| Star icon | **Optional** — bonus content, never required to progress |

**Choice nodes** let you pick ONE of two paths — both lead to the same next quest. You don't miss anything permanent.

**Cross-path quests** are optional bonuses that reward teamwork with other players on different paths. They're grindy solo but trivial if a teammate helps. **Never required.**

## Your Journey Begins Here

### Survival — *"Every empire begins with survival"*
*29 quests — your first 30 minutes*

The quest book holds your hand from punching trees to choosing your destiny. Every reward gives you exactly what the next quest needs.

```
Welcome to BOE
      |
  Punch a Tree ---> Wooden Tools ---> Cobblestone ---> Iron ---> Iron Tools
      |
  Survive the Night
     / \
 Build   Defend        <-- YOUR FIRST CHOICE!
     \ /
  Established
   /  |  \
Farm Fight Mine        <-- All 3 required
   \  |  /
Ready to Advance
      |
  Enchanting
      |
  Path Selection
  / | | | | | \
 7 Taste Tests         <-- Try every path!
  \ | | | | | /
 CHOOSE YOUR PATH      <-- Massive starter kit for ALL paths
```

**Build vs Defend** — your first big decision:
- **Build** gives you 256 stone, a door, and leather armor
- **Defend** gives you full iron armor, a bow, and 64 arrows

Both lead forward. Pick your vibe.

**Optional side quests** branch off the main line: Build a Boat, First Pet, Aquaculture, Decorate. Do them anytime or skip them entirely.

**The Nether** is optional too — explore it for bonus resources or skip straight to path selection.

## The Seven Paths

After completing Survival, seven paths open. Each has **4 ranks** with server-wide announcements when you promote. You can pursue multiple paths — nothing is locked.

---

### ⚙️ Engineering — *"Build. Automate. Dominate."*
*56 quests across 4 ranks*

The Create mod and 13 addon mods. Build factories, trains, jetpacks, and eventually nuclear-powered airships.

**Rank 1: Tinkerer** — 4 spokes radiate from the root:
- **Power:** Hand Crank → Water Wheel → Windmill → Steam Engine
- **Processing:** Andesite Casing → Press → Sheets → Crushing → Mixer → **Brass** (the gateway material!)
- **Logistics:** Belts → Funnels → Chutes
- **Kinetics:** Cogwheels → Gearbox → Clutch & Brake

All 4 converge at the **Industrialist** milestone.

**Rank 2: Industrialist** — Advanced automation:
- Assembly lines: Electron Tubes → Precision Mechanisms → Mechanical Crafters → Deployers → **Sequenced Assembly**
- Trains: Tracks → First Train → Automated Schedules
- Drilling: Drill Contraptions → Ore Excavation
- Connected: Control Chips → Kinetic Batteries → Fan Catalysts

**Rank 3: Master Engineer** — Electricity and personal gear:
- **Alternator** bridges Create (SU) with Mekanism (FE) — your windmills power laser turrets
- Enchanting machines: Blaze Enchanter → Grindstone → Forger → Printer (automated enchanting!)
- Three tiers of **jetpacks** plus a portable drill and exoskeleton
- Chunk loaders and hypertubes for infrastructure

**Rank 4: Architect of War** — The endgame:
- **Nuclear reactor** via Create New Age — thorium → fuel → reactor → unlimited power
- **Airships** via Create Aeronautics — propeller → envelope → first flight
- Drones, Netherite Jetpack, and the **Gravitron** (creative flight in survival!)
- *Optional:* Mount a cannon on your airship (requires Military path)

> *"An airship with a cannon mounted on it. Yes, really. That's the endgame."*

### ⚗️ Magic — *"Master the arcane arts"*
*69 quests across 4 ranks*

Two magic systems: **Ars Nouveau** (design custom spells from modular glyphs) and **Iron's Spells** (combat magic with boss fights).

**Rank 1: Apprentice** — Source infrastructure + your first spells:
- Source: Gems → Jars → Imbuement → Sourcelinks → Relays
- Spells: Scribe's Table → Novice Book → Your First Spell!
- **13 Tier 1 Glyphs** fan out: Projectile, Touch, Break, Harm, Heal, Ignite, Grow, Light, Freeze, Blink, Amplify, AOE...

> *Try Projectile + Harm + Amplify. Congratulations, you have a ranged nuke.*

**Rank 2: Mage** — Equipment and expanded spells:
- Enchanting Apparatus → Enchanter's Sword → Bow → Robes
- **Arcanist vs Battlemage** armor choice (more spells vs more defense)
- **12 Tier 2 Glyphs:** Lightning, Explosion, Fangs, Snare, Invisibility, Summon Wolves...
- **Companions:** Starbuncle (item transport), Wixie (auto-crafting), Drygmy (mob loot)
- Iron's Spells: Copper Book → Iron Book → first structure exploration

**Rank 3: Archmage** — Boss fights and defense:
- **Wilden Chimera** — the hardest Ars Nouveau boss
- 9 Tier 3 Glyphs: Intangible, Summon Vex, Animate Block, Wither, Glide, Rewind...
- **Spell Turrets** — auto-defense that casts spells at intruders
- Rituals: Sanctuary (no mob spawns!), Flight (creative flight!), Binding (familiars!)
- Iron's Spells bosses → **Mithril Armor**

**Rank 4: Transcendent** — Reality bends to your will:
- **Warp Portals** — permanent teleportation network
- **Ghostweave** — invisible walls (your base looks open, but it isn't)
- **Echo of Tyros** — 1000 hearts, Iron's Spells ultimate boss
- Flask Cannons — launch splash potions at enemies

### 🛡️ Military — *"Defend. Fortify. Conquer."*
*50 quests across 4 ranks*

SecurityCraft for defense, Create Big Cannons for offense. The path that ends the game.

**Rank 1: Militia** — Basic security:
- **Walls:** Reinforced Stone → Reinforced Obsidian → Electrified Fence (unbreakable by anyone but you!)
- **Cameras:** Portable Radar → Security Camera → Motion Light → Username Logger → Alarm
- **Mines:** Basic Mine → Bouncing Betty → Claymore → **IMS** (guided missiles!)  → Cage Trap

> *Reinforced Obsidian can't be broken by ANYONE but you. Encase your bed in it.*

**Rank 2: Commander** — Advanced security:
- Laser grids, inventory scanners, floor traps
- **Keypad doors** → Retinal Scanners → 5-level Keycard system
- **Sentries** (auto-turrets!) → **Trophy System** (destroys incoming projectiles!)
- Osmium Armor → **Refined Obsidian** (the strongest conventional armor)

**Rank 3: General** — Artillery:
- Cannon casting → Cast Iron → Bronze → **Steel** cannons
- **10 ammo types:** Solid, AP (pierces walls!), HE (big boom!), Shrapnel, Fluid (fill with lava!)
- **Shrapnel vs Fluid Shell** choice — area damage or area denial?
- Autocannons for rapid fire, cannon loaders for automation
- **Bed Compass** — points toward the nearest enemy bed!

**Rank 4: Warlord** — Win the game:
- **Nethersteel** cannons — the ultimate artillery
- **Block Pocket** — an impenetrable room only you can enter. Put your bed here.
- **Bed Destroyer** → **Last Bed Standing** = **WIN THE GAME**
- *Optional:* Nuclear Arsenal (requires Alchemy's fusion reactor)

### 🌾 Economy — *"Farm. Store. Automate."*
*46 quests across 4 ranks*

Mystical Agriculture grows resources from seeds. AE2 stores them digitally. Together, you never run out.

**Rank 1: Farmer** — Storage and seeds:
- **Storage Drawers:** Drawers → Controller → Compacting → Upgrades → Void
- **Mystical Agriculture:** Inferium Essence → Seed Base → First Resource Seed → Growth Accelerators
- Sophisticated Backpacks: Base → Copper → Iron → Crafting Upgrade

> *Growth Accelerators STACK. Put 4 under farmland for insane crop speed.*

**Rank 2: Merchant** — Digital storage:
- **AE2:** Certus Quartz → Controller → Drives → Cells → Crafting Terminal → Import/Export
- **Seed Tiers:** Prudentium → Tertium → Infusion Altar → **Soulium Dagger** (farm mob drops!)
- Gold Backpack + Feeding Upgrade (auto-eat!)

**Rank 3: Tycoon** — Autocrafting:
- AE2 Patterns → Assemblers → 16k/64k Storage → **Wireless Terminal** (access storage anywhere!)
- Imperium → **Supremium** (grow ANYTHING — diamonds, netherite, anything)
- **Create Applied Kinetics** — bridges Create factories with AE2 storage
- Chunk Loading — keep farms running while you're away

**Rank 4: Magnate** — Full automation:
- **Awakened Supremium** — the ultimate essence tier
- Seeds grow → Harvester collects → AE2 stores → Autocrafting produces → you never touch a resource again
- Spatial Storage — store entire ROOMS digitally

### 🔬 Alchemy — *"Transmute. Transform. Transcend."*
*49 quests across 4 ranks*

Mekanism ore processing, nuclear power, and eventually the MekaSuit. Plus Apotheosis world tiers for RPG loot.

**Rank 1: Apprentice Alchemist** — Ore multiplication:
- **Ore chain:** 2x (Enrichment) → 3x (Purification) → 4x (Injection) → eventually 5x!
- Circuits: Basic → Advanced (each tier unlocks better machines)
- Power: Heat → Solar → Wind generators

**Rank 2: Transmuter** — Gas processing and factories:
- Chemical Oxidizer → Separator → Infuser → Reaction Chamber → Condensentrator
- Factories: Basic (3 slots) → Advanced (5) → **Elite** (7 parallel slots!)
- **Digital Miner** — automatically extracts specific ores from underground
- **Apotheosis World Tiers:** Haven → Frontier → Ascent (stronger enemies, better loot)

**Rank 3: Nuclear Alchemist** — Nuclear power:
- Ultimate Circuits → **Fission Reactor** → Turbine → **FUSION REACTOR** (unlimited energy!)
- Quantum Entangloporter — wireless transfer of anything across any distance
- Mekanism Laser — concentrated energy beam weapon

**Rank 4: Philosopher** — Iron Man:
- **MekaSuit:** Helmet → Body (+ Elytra!) → Pants → Boots → Full Set = you ARE the endgame
- **Meka-Tool:** One tool does everything. Vein Mining + Teleportation modules.
- **SPS:** Supercritical Phase Shifter — converts polonium to antimatter

> *Full MekaSuit with all modules = flight, teleportation, vein mining, damage immunity.*

### 🏠 Housewife — *"Cook. Decorate. Nurture."*
*49 quests across 4 ranks*

Food buffs win fights. The Housewife keeps everyone fed, buffed, and living in style.

**Rank 1: Homemaker** — Kitchen setup:
- Stove → Cutting Board → Cooking Pot → Skillet → Diamond Knife
- Tomato, Cabbage, Onion, Rice, Rich Soil
- **Beef Stew** (Comfort = regeneration!) → **Steak & Potatoes** (Nourishment = no hunger drain!)

> *Comfort and Nourishment are HUGE in PvP. Fed fighters win.*

**Rank 2: Chef** — Advanced cooking and drinks:
- **Feasts:** Roast Chicken → Honey Glazed Ham → Gleaming Salad (placeable shared meals!)
- **Winery:** Grape Farming → Wine Cellar → Cabernet Sauvignon → Champagne (Resistance buff! ...20% Nausea risk)
- **Confectionery:** Chocolate Bars → Candy → **Soothing Hot Chocolate** (Regeneration II!)
- **Seafood:** Guardian Tail → Tentacles → Guardian Soup

> *Each chocolate type = different buff: Dark=Haste, White=Rest, Ruby=Saturation, Caramel=Speed*

**Rank 3: Interior Designer** — Decoration mastery:
- **Chipped:** 6 workstations, hundreds of decorative block variants
- **Macaw's:** Windows (with shape-changing hammer!) → Doors → Fences → Trapdoors
- **Create Furniture:** Chairs, tables, pillars, catwalks
- **Support Logistics:** Feeding Upgrade + Alchemy Upgrade = auto-buff backpack

**Rank 4: Matriarch** — Automated cooking and team support:
- **Candy Factory** — auto-produce combat chocolates via Create
- **Gingerbread Architecture** — build walls from edible gingerbread that gives Resistance!
- **Netherite Backpack** with Everlasting Upgrade — survives death
- **Grand Feast** — 5 feast blocks for the ultimate dining hall

### 👁️ Scout — *"Explore. Infiltrate. Sabotage."*
*51 quests across 4 ranks*

Dungeon-crawling for legendary boss weapons. Base infiltration for intel. Sabotage to cripple enemies.

**Rank 1: Pathfinder** — Exploration tools:
- **Structure Compass** — finds any structure in the world
- **Steampunk Helmet** — built-in RADAR, see entities through walls!
- **Wither vs Divine armor choice** — Wither curses attackers, Divine deflects one hit/minute
- Cataclysm Eyes — 8 types, each locates a different dungeon
- Apothic Enchanting: Hellshelf → Seashelf → Deepshelf → Endshelf → Library → Extraction Tome

**Rank 2: Infiltrator** — Boss fights and intel:
- **5 Cataclysm bosses** in order: Netherite Monstrosity (9,600 HP!) → Ender Guardian → Ignis → Harbinger → Ancient Remnant
- **Copy Paste Gadget** — scan enemy base layouts! Bring the template back to your team.
- **Recon Mission** → Blueprint Analysis → Destruction Gadget
- **Apotheosis:** Gem Cutting → Socketing → Salvaging → Reforging → Augmenting

> *The Copy Paste Gadget is the core intel mechanic. Copy their base, share the template, plan the attack.*

**Rank 3: Saboteur** — Sabotage and advanced bosses:
- **Sabotage chain:** Plant Mines → Cut Power → Destroy Infrastructure → Deep Cover
- **4 more bosses:** Maledictus (8,400 HP!) → Leviathan (underwater!) → Scylla → Weapon Fusion
- Draconic Endshelf → Library of Alexandria → Occult Ender Lead (capture ANY entity!)
- Neptunium armor for underwater boss hunts

**Rank 4: Shadow** — Legendary gear:
- **Ignitium Armor** — combines with Elytra! Flying scout in legendary armor.
- **Cursium Armor** — Ghost Vision (see through walls), Undead Revive (cheat death), Ghost Dodge
- **Shoulder Weapons** — auto-firing missiles mounted on your shoulder
- **The Cataclysmfarer** — defeat ALL Cataclysm bosses → **Legendary Arsenal** (5 boss weapons!)

> *Ignitium Elytra Chestplate + Lava Walker Boots + Gaze of Heat Helmet. Flying, lava-walking legend.*
