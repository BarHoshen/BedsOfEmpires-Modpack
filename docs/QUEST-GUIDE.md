# Beds of Empires — Quest Guide

> *Your bed is your life. Protect it, or die trying.*

Welcome to **Beds of Empires**, a PvP modpack where players defend their bed, progress through Civilization-inspired tech trees, and destroy enemy beds to win. This guide covers all quests across **8 chapters** (1 tutorial + 7 paths).

---

## How Quests Work

Open your **Quest Book** to see your available quests. Here's how to read the quest map:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Large hexagon | **Starting point** or **final achievement** — look here first |
| Small hexagon | **Rank milestone** — completing this promotes you and announces it server-wide |
| Circle | Normal quest — complete to unlock the next |
| Rounded square | Special item (glyphs, optional content) |
| Greyed out quest | **Locked** — complete its parent quest first |
| Star icon | **Optional** — bonus content, never required to progress |

**Choice nodes** let you pick ONE of two paths — both lead to the same next quest. You don't miss anything permanent.

**Cross-path quests** are optional bonuses that reward teamwork with other players on different paths. They're grindy solo but trivial if a teammate helps. They never appear before Rank 2.

## Defense — Every Path Defends Itself

Every main path has a way to defend its base, with Military as the reference. Cross-path help from Steadkeeper (humanoid guards) is always available.

| Path | Defense Method | When |
|------|----------------|------|
| Survival | Iron Golem + Arrow Dispenser network | Late optional branch |
| Engineering | Mechanical Saw + Crushing Wheel + Encased Fan + Deployer Sentinel | Rank 3 |
| Magic | Ars Nouveau Spell Turrets + Ars Elemental focus | Rank 1-2 |
| Military | Full SecurityCraft + Create Big Cannons | All ranks |
| Alchemy | Mekanism Laser + Laser Amplifier + Tractor Beam | Rank 2 |
| Expeditioner | Personal PvP mastery — *they ARE the defense* | All ranks |
| Steadkeeper | Guard Villagers + Iron Golems (sells defense to others) | Rank 2-3 |
| Logistics | Supplies and refuels other paths' defenses | Cross-path |

## Your Journey Begins Here

### Survival — *"Every empire begins with survival"*
*Your first 30 minutes*

The quest book holds your hand from punching trees to choosing your destiny. Every reward gives you exactly what the next quest needs.

```
Welcome to BOE
      |
  Punch a Tree ---> Wooden Tools ---> Cobblestone ---> Iron ---> Iron Tools
      |
  Survive the Night
     / \
 Build   Defend        <-- YOUR FIRST CHOICE!
     \ /
  Established
   /  |  \
Farm Fight Mine        <-- All 3 required
   \  |  /
Ready to Advance
      |
  Enchanting
      |
  Path Selection
  / | | | | | \
 7 Taste Tests         <-- Try every path!
  \ | | | | | /
 CHOOSE YOUR PATH      <-- Massive starter kit for ALL paths
```

**Build vs Defend** — your first big decision:
- **Build** gives you 256 stone, a door, and leather armor
- **Defend** gives you full iron armor, a bow, and 64 arrows

Both lead forward. Pick your vibe.

**Optional side quests** branch off the main line: Build a Boat, First Pet, Aquaculture, Decorate. Do them anytime or skip them entirely.

**The Nether** is optional too — explore it for bonus resources or skip straight to path selection.

**NEW: Optional Defense Branch** at the end — Iron Stockpile → Carved Pumpkin → Iron Golem → Dispenser → Arrow Volley → Tripwire Trigger → Defense Perimeter. Costlier than the main line, but gives Survival players a way to protect their bed without choosing Military.

## The Seven Paths

After completing Survival, seven paths open. Each has **4 ranks** with server-wide announcements when you promote. You can pursue multiple paths — nothing is locked.

---

### ⚙️ Engineering — *"Build. Automate. Dominate."*

The Create mod and 13 addon mods. Build factories, trains, jetpacks, and eventually nuclear-powered airships.

**Rank 1: Tinkerer** — 4 spokes radiate from the root:
- **Power:** Hand Crank → Water Wheel → Windmill → Steam Engine
- **Processing:** Andesite Casing → Press → Sheets → Crushing → Mixer → **Brass** (the gateway material!)
- **Logistics:** Belts → Funnels → Chutes
- **Kinetics:** Cogwheels → Gearbox → Clutch & Brake

All 4 converge at the **Industrialist** milestone.

**Rank 2: Industrialist** — Advanced automation:
- Assembly lines: Electron Tubes → Precision Mechanisms → Mechanical Crafters → Deployers → **Sequenced Assembly**
- Trains: Tracks → First Train → Automated Schedules
- Drilling: Drill Contraptions → Ore Excavation
- **NEW: Sophisticated Storage** — Chest → Iron Chest → Storage Controller (the Engineer's storage solution)

**Rank 3: Master Engineer** — Defense and electricity:
- **Alternator** bridges Create (SU) with Mekanism (FE) — your windmills power laser turrets
- Enchanting machines: Blaze Enchanter → Grindstone → Forger → Printer (automated enchanting!)
- Three tiers of **jetpacks** plus a portable drill and exoskeleton
- Chunk loaders and hypertubes for infrastructure
- **NEW: Create Defense Chain** — Mechanical Saw → Crushing Wheel Corridor → Encased Fan Fire Trap → Deployer Sentinel
- *Cross-path:* XP from Expeditioner boss fights powers Create Enchantment Industry

**Rank 4: Architect of War** — The endgame:
- **Nuclear reactor** via Create New Age — thorium → fuel → reactor → unlimited power
- **Airships** via Create Aeronautics — propeller → envelope → first flight
- Drones, Netherite Jetpack, and the **Gravitron** (creative flight in survival!)
- *Optional:* Mount a cannon on your airship (requires Military path)

> *"An airship with a cannon mounted on it. Yes, really. That's the endgame."*

### ⚗️ Magic — *"Two schools, one mastery"*

Two magic systems: **Ars Nouveau** (utility, automation, glyphs) and **Iron's Spells 'n Spellbooks** (combat magic, spell books, school-specific armor). Plus **Ars Elemental** for fire/water/earth/air focus.

**Rank 1: Apprentice** — Source infrastructure + your first spells:
- Source: Gems → Jars → Imbuement → Sourcelinks → Relays
- Spells: Scribe's Table → Novice Book → **Apprentice Spellbook** → Your First Spell!
- **13 Tier 1 Glyphs** fan out: Projectile, Touch, Break, Harm, Heal, Ignite, Grow, Light, Freeze, Blink, Amplify, AOE...

> *Try Projectile + Harm + Amplify. Congratulations, you have a ranged nuke.*

**Rank 2: Mage** — Equipment, defense, and storage:
- Enchanting Apparatus → Enchanter's Sword → Bow → Robes
- **Arcanist vs Battlemage** armor choice (more spells vs more defense)
- **Spell Turrets** — auto-defense that casts spells at intruders (Magic's defense solution)
- **NEW: Storage Lectern + Bookwyrm Charm** — the mage's library and automated organization
- Companions: Starbuncle, Wixie, Drygmy
- Iron's Spells: Inscription Table → First Scroll → Copper/Iron Spell Books

**Rank 3: Archmage** — Boss fights and combat magic:
- **Wilden Chimera** — the hardest Ars Nouveau boss
- 9 Tier 3 Glyphs: Intangible, Summon Vex, Animate Block, Wither, Glide, Rewind...
- Rituals: Sanctuary, Flight, Binding (familiars!)
- Iron's Spells: **Wizard's Robes** + raw mithril → mithril ingots
- *Cross-path:* Boss-dropped spell book from Expeditioner unlocks deeper Iron's Spells content

**Rank 4: Transcendent** — Reality bends to your will:
- **Warp Portals** — permanent teleportation network
- **Ghostweave** — invisible walls (your base looks open, but it isn't)
- Ars Elemental: Fire/Water/Earth/Air focus mastery
- Iron's Spells: **Netherite Spell Book** → **Netherite Mage Armor** (apex spellcaster gear)
- Flask Cannons — launch splash potions at enemies

### 🛡️ Military — *"Defend. Fortify. Conquer."*

SecurityCraft for defense, Create Big Cannons for offense. The path that ends the game.

**Rank 1: Militia** — Basic security:
- **Walls:** Reinforced Stone → Reinforced Obsidian → Electrified Fence (unbreakable by anyone but you!)
- **Cameras:** Portable Radar → Security Camera → Motion Light → Username Logger → Alarm
- **Mines:** Basic Mine → Bouncing Betty → Claymore → **IMS** (guided missiles!) → Cage Trap

> *Reinforced Obsidian can't be broken by ANYONE but you. Encase your bed in it.*

**Rank 2: Commander** — Advanced security and storage:
- Laser grids, inventory scanners, floor traps
- **Keypad doors** → Retinal Scanners → 5-level Keycard system
- **Sentries** (auto-turrets!) → **Trophy System** (destroys incoming projectiles!)
- **NEW: Storage Drawers — Munitions Depot** (Military's storage solution)
- **NEW: More Bows** — Copper → Diamond → Blaze/Amethyst specialty bows
- **NEW: Shield Variants** — Iron → Diamond → Netherite shields
- *Cross-path:* Steadkeeper's Beef Stew + Steak & Potatoes give the Soldier sustained combat nutrition

**Rank 3: General** — Artillery:
- Cannon casting → Cast Iron → Bronze → **Steel** cannons
- **10 ammo types:** Solid, AP (pierces walls!), HE (big boom!), Shrapnel, Fluid (fill with lava!)
- **Shrapnel vs Fluid Shell** choice — area damage or area denial?
- Autocannons for rapid fire, cannon loaders for automation
- **Bed Compass** — points toward the nearest enemy bed!
- *Cross-path:* Engineering's Precision Mechanisms power Big Cannon targeting; Expeditioner's Apotheosis gems socket into armor

**Rank 4: Warlord** — Win the game:
- **Nethersteel** cannons — the ultimate artillery
- **Block Pocket** — an impenetrable room only you can enter. Put your bed here.
- **Bed Destroyer** → **Last Bed Standing** = **WIN THE GAME**
- *Optional:* Nuclear Arsenal (requires Alchemy's fusion reactor)

### ⛓ Logistics — *"Every item. In its place. On demand."*

The master of item movement and storage networks. Where Engineering builds machines, Logistics manages flow. Centered on **AE2** with **Hypertube** and **Railway Navigator** for cross-map cargo, ending in the **AE2 Quantum Bridge**.

**Rank 1: Logistician** — Local storage:
- **Storage Drawers:** Drawers → Controller → Compacting → Upgrades → Void
- **Sophisticated Backpacks**: Base → Copper → Iron → Crafting Upgrade
- **AE2 Foundations:** Certus Quartz → Charger → Inscriber

> *Storage Drawers are the gateway. AE2 is the goal.*

**Rank 2: Networker** — ME network and Hypertube transit:
- **AE2:** Controller → Drives → Cells → Crafting Terminal → Import/Export buses
- **NEW: Hypertube Network** — high-speed player transit between bases (carry goods at speed)
- *Cross-path:* AE2 meteorites = Sky Stone, found by exploring → Expeditioner trade

**Rank 3: Curator** — Auto-crafting and Railway Navigator:
- AE2 Patterns → Pattern Provider → Molecular Assembler → 16k/64k Storage
- **Wireless Terminal** — access storage anywhere within range
- **NEW: Railway Navigator** — schedule cargo trains, plan automated supply routes
- *Cross-path:* Engineering's Create Applied Kinetics bridges Create rotation to AE2 channels

**Rank 4: Architect** — Quantum-distance logistics:
- **Quantum Network Bridge** — link ME networks across any distance, even across dimensions
- **Wireless Crafting Terminal** — craft remotely from anywhere
- **Trading Floor** — automate villager trade networks
- *Cross-path:* Applied Mekanistics connects Alchemy's chemical pipelines to the ME Network

> *Logistics doesn't fight battles. Logistics decides who has the supplies to fight them.*

### 🔬 Alchemy — *"Master matter and energy"*

Mekanism ore processing, nuclear power, antimatter forging, and the **QIO storage system**. The chemist's path. Plus Apotheosis world tiers for RPG loot. **MekaSuit removed.**

**Rank 1: Initiate** — Ore multiplication:
- **Ore chain:** 2x (Enrichment) → 3x (Purification) → 4x (Injection) → eventually 5x!
- Circuits: Basic → Advanced (each tier unlocks better machines)
- Power: Heat → Solar → Wind generators

**Rank 2: Transmuter** — Gas processing, factories, and defense:
- Chemical Oxidizer → Separator → Infuser → Reaction Chamber → Condensentrator
- Factories: Basic → Advanced → **Elite** (7 parallel slots!)
- **Digital Miner** — automatically extracts specific ores from underground
- **NEW: Mekanism Laser Defense** — Laser → Laser Amplifier → Laser Tractor Beam (Alchemy's defense)
- *Cross-path:* Bio Fuel from Steadkeeper food powers the Bio Generator

**Rank 3: Nuclear Alchemist** — Nuclear power and storage:
- Ultimate Circuits → **Fission Reactor** → Turbine → **FUSION REACTOR** (unlimited energy!)
- **NEW: QIO Storage System** — QIO Drive Array → Dashboard → Drives → Importers/Exporters (Alchemy's storage solution)
- **NEW: Hazmat Suit** — Mask → Gown → Pants → Boots (radiation protection, the chemist's armor)
- *Cross-path:* MystAg-grown Supremium essence from Expeditioner provides bypass for ore farming

**Rank 4: Master Alchemist** — Antimatter:
- **SPS:** Supercritical Phase Shifter — converts polonium to plutonium to **antimatter**
- **Antiprotonic Nucleosynthesizer** — the apex of Mekanism science
- **Atomic Disassembler** + Jetpack + Free Runners + HDPE Elytra — the Alchemist's mobility kit
- *Cross-path:* Applied Mekanistics ME Chemical Cells connect to the Logistics ME Network

> *No MekaSuit. Just chemistry, fusion, and antimatter — the real endgame.*

### ✿ Steadkeeper — *"The hearth is the heart of victory"*

The home-builder, the cook, the buff-provider. Without a Steadkeeper, the team doesn't eat, drink, or live in style. **Now also the team's potion brewer and humanoid guard recruiter.**

**Rank 1: Homemaker** — Kitchen and pantry:
- Stove → Cutting Board → Cooking Pot → Skillet → Diamond Knife
- Tomato, Cabbage, Onion, Rice, Rich Soil
- **Beef Stew** (Comfort = regeneration!) → **Steak & Potatoes** (Nourishment = no hunger drain!)
- **NEW: Storage Drawers** — organized pantry (Steadkeeper's storage solution)
- **NEW: Goblin Trader Trade** — find a goblin in caves, get unique wares

> *Comfort and Nourishment are HUGE in PvP. Fed fighters win.*

**Rank 2: Artisan** — Advanced cooking, building, defense, potions:
- **Feasts:** Roast Chicken → Honey Glazed Ham → Gleaming Salad (placeable shared meals!)
- **Winery:** Grape Farming → Wine Cellar → Cabernet Sauvignon → Champagne (Resistance buff!)
- **Confectionery:** Chocolate Bars → Candy → **Soothing Hot Chocolate** (Regeneration II!)
- **NEW: Building Gadgets 2** — Building/Exchanging/Destruction/Copy-Paste gadgets (moved here from Scout)
- **NEW: Slice & Dice** — Create + Farmer's Delight industrial cooking
- **NEW: Defense — Guard Villagers + Iron Golem** (Steadkeeper's living defenders, sold to other paths)
- **NEW: Potions branch** — Brewing Stand → Splash Potion → Lingering → Potion Blending Stand → Blended Potions

**Rank 3: Castellan** — Decoration mastery:
- **Chipped:** 6 workstations, hundreds of decorative block variants
- **Macaw's:** Windows (with shape-changing hammer!) → Doors → Fences → Trapdoors
- **Create Furniture:** Chairs, tables, pillars, catwalks
- *Cross-path:* MystAg essence from Expeditioner unlocks high-tier potion ingredients

**Rank 4: Grandmaster** — Automated cooking and ultimate buffs:
- **Candy Factory** — auto-produce combat chocolates via Create
- **Gingerbread Architecture** — build walls from edible gingerbread that gives Resistance!
- **Netherite Backpack** with Everlasting Upgrade — survives death
- **Grand Feast** — 5 feast blocks for the ultimate dining hall

### ⚔ Expeditioner — *"Hunt. Conquer. Dominate."*

The apex melee PvP fighter and boss hunter. Cataclysm bosses, Apotheosis loot, and rare materials grown via Mystical Agriculture. The Expeditioner is the team's solo carry — and at endgame, the most feared melee fighter on the server.

**Rank 1: Wanderer** — Exploration tools:
- **Structure Compass** — finds any structure in the world
- **Steampunk Helmet** — built-in RADAR, see entities through walls!
- **NEW: Sophisticated Backpacks** — Base → Iron → Diamond (Expeditioner's storage solution)
- **NEW: Better Archaeology** — unearthing ancient treasures
- Cataclysm Eyes — 8 types, each locates a different dungeon
- Apothic Enchanting: Hellshelf → Seashelf → Deepshelf → Endshelf → Library

**Rank 2: Hunter** — Boss fights and Mystical Agriculture:
- **5 Cataclysm bosses** in order: Netherite Monstrosity (9,600 HP!) → Ender Guardian → Ignis → Harbinger → Ancient Remnant
- **NEW: Mystical Agriculture full progression** — Inferium → Prudentium → Tertium → Imperium → Supremium → **Awakened Supremium** → **Nether Star Shard** (you can GROW nether stars!)
- **Apotheosis:** Gem Cutting → Socketing → Salvaging → Reforging → Augmenting

> *The Expeditioner's MystAg farm is the team's resource pool. Trade essences with other paths.*

**Rank 3: Slayer** — Sabotage and advanced bosses:
- **4 more bosses:** Maledictus (8,400 HP!) → Leviathan (underwater!) → Scylla → Weapon Fusion
- Draconic Endshelf → Library of Alexandria → Occult Ender Lead (capture ANY entity!)
- **NEW: Trim Effects + Easy Anvils + Disenchanting Table** (the Expeditioner's gear cycling rig)
- *Cross-path optional:* Iron's Spells diamond_spell_book — combat magic for boss fights

**Rank 4: Warlord** — Legendary gear:
- **Ignitium Armor** — combines with Elytra! Flying scout in legendary armor.
- **Cursium Armor** — Ghost Vision (see through walls), Undead Revive (cheat death), Ghost Dodge
- **Shoulder Weapons** — auto-firing missiles mounted on your shoulder
- **The Cataclysmfarer** — defeat ALL Cataclysm bosses → **Legendary Arsenal** (5 boss weapons!)
- *Building Gadgets moved out — they're the Steadkeeper's domain now.*
